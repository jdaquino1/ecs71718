Malala Yousafzai

Malala

Malala is an activist that is fighting for girls education.
She won a Nobel Peace Prize at the age of 17 for her work as an advocate.
That is just two years after she was shot in the head by the Taliban and survived.

Wikipedia Page
  • 2011 - She was awarded Pakistan's first Youth National Peace Prize
  • 2012- Malala spoke out against the Taliban.
  • 2013 - She published her book.
  • 2014 - Malala was named one of the Most Influential Teens of 2014.
  • 2016 - Malala was an advocate for Syrian refugees.
